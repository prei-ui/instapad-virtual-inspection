Step 1

Take Your Photos and Video

Follow these rules for every photo and your video walkthrough. Quality and framing matter — poor lighting or blurry photos may require a reshoot.

📷
Photo Rules
2–4 photos per room, landscape orientation

Take 2–4 photos per room and keep every photo in landscape orientation (horizontal, not vertical). Stand back so we can see the full space — wide angle or 0.5x zoom is ideal.

✅Landscape / horizontal orientation
❌Portrait / vertical photos
✅Good lighting — open blinds, turn on lights
❌Dark, blurry, or overexposed shots
✅Wide angle — show the full room
❌Close-up shots that cut off the space
✅Daylight preferred for exterior shots
❌No need to clean or stage — just clear visibility
🎥
Video Walkthrough — Required
One continuous video, inside and outside

Record a steady, continuous walkthrough video with good lighting. This is in addition to your photos — not a replacement for them.

📋
How to record it: Start outside with a full exterior walk-around (front, both sides, back), then walk through every room inside — living areas, kitchen, bedrooms, bathrooms, hallways, laundry, and any bonus spaces. Move slowly and pan the camera so we can clearly see walls, ceilings, floors, and any visible damage or repairs.

Step 3

Create a Google Photos Album

We use Google Photos for photo collection. Follow these steps to create your album and get a shareable link.

1
Open Google Photos on your phone or computer
2
Tap Library then Albums then New Album
3
Name the album — suggested: 123 Main St – Virtual Inspection
4
Add all your photos and your walkthrough video to the album
5
Tap Share then Create link then Copy link
6
Head to the Submit Photos tab and paste your link — you are done!
